HIGHLIGHT OF THE OPENING DAY

10.45 AM

Aalborg Gardens Nisseorkester enterains

11.00 AM

Santa's ship docks at Honnørkajen. Santa then greets all the childen

11.45 AM

Mayor Thomas Kastrip-Larsen gives a welcome speech to Santa Claus

12.00 PM

Santa Claus and the mayor drive in a sleigh through the city streets

14.30 PM

Procession from Gabels Torv with Aalborg Garden, Santa Claus, Mayor Thomas Kastrup- Larsen and representative from Fredrikstad Siri Martinsen, in the emergency services’ old fire truck.

15.00 PM

Aalborg Garden plays by the Christmas tree.

15.30

The Christmas tree is lit. Subsequently, mulled wine and apple slices are served in front of Aalborg City Hall.
