Christmas market returns!

The most asked questions

What are the opening hours of Christmas Market? Where can I find it?

Christmas Market is opened from 19.11 until 23.12 and take place in Gammeltorv and Nytorv. You can learn more about opening hours here

Are there going to be any food and drinks at Christmas Market?

Can you imagine a Christmas Market without any snacks? Definitely no! At our market you’ll find various winter drinks including gløgg and hot chocolate. You should also try our home-made cakes, candies, roasted mandlers or hot dogs, which you can find on the stalls.

What kind of stalls are going to be at the Market?

At Aalborg’s Christmas Market you can find delicious handcraft food and beverages as well as warm winter clothes, such as Christmas sweaters.
